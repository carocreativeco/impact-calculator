Why This Matters
This tool helps franchise owners understand the relationship between review volume and average rating. As the number of total reviews grows, it generally takes more positive reviews to create meaningful movement in the overall GPA. By making that relationship visible, the calculator helps translate reputation goals into concrete review targets that can support local outreach, customer follow-up, service recovery, and broader reputation strategy.
